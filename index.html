Privacy & Data Disclosure

1. Data Collection & Identity
Upon entry, we collect your Full Name, Professional Email, IP Address, and Access Timestamp. This data is transmitted securely to metrics@joinfoundry.run.

2. Purpose of Processing
This data is used exclusively to maintain a Confidentiality Audit Trail. It ensures that the proprietary research and "In-Progress" ventures (e.g., LifeMetric, Fintech Frameworks) are protected under a verifiable Non-Disclosure agreement.

3. Legal Basis (GDPR/NDPR)
Processing is carried out under Legitimate Interest to protect Intellectual Property and Trade Secrets, and Contractual Necessity established by your agreement to the site's access terms.

4. Data Retention & Sharing
Logs are retained for the duration of the project's development cycle. We do not sell your data or share it with third parties, except where required by law to enforce intellectual property rights.

5. Your Rights
You may contact the Data Protection Officer at osanya@joinfoundry.run to request a copy of your logged data or its deletion, provided such a request does not compromise the security of the audit trail.
